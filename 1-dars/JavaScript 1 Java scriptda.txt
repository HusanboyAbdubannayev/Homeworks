  ==> JavaScriptda ikki xil data type bor (Primitive va Non-Primitive)

             |--> let a = 0; console.log(Number.MAX_SAFE_INTEGER); eng katta sonni aniqlaberadi bu sondan balantida hisob kitob natogri bld  (9007199254740991)
